## About Me

#me



I am **Mahmood Hashemi,** a passionate *software engineer* and computer science student from the historic city of Herat, Afghanistan.

### Background

With a deep love for technology and problem-solving, I've dedicated myself to mastering the art of software development while pursuing my academic goals. Growing up in Herat, a city known for its rich cultural heritage and architectural marvels, has inspired me to blend traditional values with modern technological innovations.

### Professional Focus

As a software engineer, I work on various projects that challenge my skills and push me to learn new technologies constantly. My journey in computer science has equipped me with a strong foundation in:

- Programming
- Algorithms
- System Design

### Mission

I am committed to contributing to the tech community and aspire to use my knowledge and skills to make a positive impact, particularly in developing technological solutions that can benefit my community and beyond.
